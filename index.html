Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how the platform works.

How do I get started on Funngro?
1. Sign Up : Download the app, verify with OTP, and get started in minutes.
2. Choose a Campaign : Browse and join brand campaigns that match your interests.
3. Complete & Earn : Finish the campaign and receive instant payments via UPI or bank transfer.
Is Funngro free to use?
Yes, Funngro is completely free. We do not charge any registration fees or hidden costs for applying to projects.
How and when do I get paid?
Once your submitted work is approved by the brand, the agreed amount is credited to your Funngro wallet immediately. You can withdraw this to your bank account or UPI at any time.
What is a Toffee project?
Toffee Projects are simple tasks like surveys, playing games, or app downloads. They are quick-earning tasks with fast approval, easy income, and you can do unlimited Toffee Projects.
